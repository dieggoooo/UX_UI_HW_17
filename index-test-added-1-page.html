My Name is Diego Cerezo

I started off as a Graphic Designer which I fell in love with when I was a freshman in highscool. My love grew more and more throughout the years and ended up getting my first internship when I was 17 as a graphic designer at Piedmont Hospital.

After I was finished with that internship, I wanted to take my chances and apply for a Graphic Design position as a car wrap designer.

After I soon moved to college, I had to quit my car wrapping poition but I did start majoring in Graphic Design. This is when I started learning about what being a Ux and Ui is and figured I would also try it.

All that had lead up to this moment where I am at right now, learning more and more everday about what it takes to be a Ux and Ui Designer.
